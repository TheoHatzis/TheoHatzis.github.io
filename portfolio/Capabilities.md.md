# Capabilities

*   **Electronics Hardware Design & Development**
    *   Expert in mixed-signal circuit design, including analogue, digital, high-voltage, low-voltage, temperature, and current sensing applications.
    *   Proficient in schematic capture and PCB layout using Altium Designer, Mentor Graphics Expedition, and OrCAD, adhering to IPC standards and best practices.
    *   Skilled in integrating input protections, EMI/ESD filters, and power supply blocks, including DC/DC converters and power management units.
    *   Experienced in designing 8/16/32-bit microcontroller and microprocessor-based systems, incorporating digital communication protocols such as CAN, RS485, and RS232.
    *   Adept at developing detailed design descriptions, performing FMEA and MTBF calculations, and generating comprehensive analysis reports.
    *   Competent in managing thermal and mechanical considerations for robust hardware enclosures and system integration.
*   **Semiconductor Test & Post-Silicon Char/Verification/Validation**
    *   Specialist in developing and executing comprehensive qualification and verification test plans (QTP, VTP, ATP) for semiconductor devices.
    *   Highly experienced in post-silicon validation and characterisation of DC/DC converters, PMICs, and high-voltage SiC/IGBT gate drivers, including static and dynamic measurements.
    *   Proficient in PVT (Process, Voltage, Temperature) characterisation of mixed-signal devices and RF Front End components.
    *   Skilled in debugging, de-risking, and integrating PCBs at both module and system levels, including SoC bring-up.
    *   Adept at performing detailed fault analysis (FMEDA) and ensuring alignment with Product Requirements (PRQ) and datasheets.
    *   Experienced in wafer-level testing and characterisation using advanced probers and SMU/MUX setups for MEMS ISFETs and other devices.
    *   Competent in conducting environmental and functional testing in accordance with aerospace standards such as RTCA/DO-160.
*   **Embedded Systems**
    *   Extensive experience in designing and implementing 8/16/32-bit embedded microprocessor and microcontroller applications.
    *   Proficient in programming microcontrollers in C, including configuring peripheral interfaces like SPI, I2C, and UART.
    *   Skilled in developing low-power CPU and CPLD designs, incorporating battery-backed RAM, watchdog timers, and power management.
    *   Experienced in FPGA-based development boards, including power distribution and core supply design for complex systems.
    *   Familiar with JTAG, Lauterbach Power Trace32, logic analysers, and I2C analysers for comprehensive embedded system debugging.
    *   Capable of designing high-speed memory bus interfaces and managing clock control for optimal system performance.
*   **Test Automation & Measurement**
    *   Expert in developing and implementing automated test processes using Python, MATLAB, and TestStand for various lab instruments and PXI systems.
    *   Proficient in controlling bench equipment via PyVISA, C#/.NET, and LabVIEW, including SMUs, oscilloscopes, spectrum analysers, and signal generators.
    *   Skilled in collecting, processing, and analysing large volumes of test data, utilising libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, and Plotly for visualisation.
    *   Experienced in designing and configuring test benches, including custom jigs and high-speed dynamic load forcing circuits.
    *   Adept at documenting test procedures, results, and technical specifications for internal reference and customer reports.
    *   Competent in performing precise measurements for ripple, efficiency, load/line transients, startup modes, and quiescent current.
    *   Familiar with version control systems like GIT for managing test software and engineering data.
*   **Wireless & RF Systems**
    *   Extensive knowledge and experience with RF circuits, including amplifiers, filters, mixers, oscillators, and digital PLLs, along with relevant measurement techniques.
    *   Proficient in developing and testing 24GHz and 60GHz Radar sensors, including SDK and GUI test software.
    *   Experienced in the verification of 2G, 3G, and 4G mobile platform chipsets and modems, including RF transceiver and power amplifier (PA) measurements.
    *   Skilled in characterising digital interfaces such as SPI, I2C, and UART, crucial for RF system integration.
    *   Adept at antenna matching, tuning, and performance optimisation for wireless devices.
    *   Competent in analysing noise emissions and spread-spectrum modulation in RF systems.
*   **Medical Devices & Instrumentation**
    *   Experienced in the design and development of safety-critical hardware for the medical sector, including infusion pumps and diagnostic equipment.
    *   Skilled in designing electronics for medical imaging systems such as gamma cameras, NMR, and XRF instruments.
    *   Proficient in developing sensor conditioning and interface circuits for various medical and scientific applications.
    *   Adept at ensuring product compliance with relevant safety and regulatory standards (e.g., CE, UL) for medical devices.
    *   Experienced in data acquisition systems for patient monitoring and analytical instrumentation.
    *   Capable of performing functional and environmental testing to ensure reliability and performance in clinical settings.
    *   Involved in the design of high-precision electronics for scientific instruments used in materials analysis.
*   **Highways ITS, Informatics, Alerts, and Signalling**
    *   Experienced in designing communications infrastructure equipment for controlled motorways, including ruggedised 19-inch rack systems for roadside outstations.
    *   Proficient in implementing digital communication protocols such as RS485, Vb, and Vb for long-distance copper circuits, including galvanic isolation and lightning protection.
    *   Skilled in developing traffic data acquisition equipment, including inductive loop detectors, Weigh-In-Motion (WIM) systems, and magnetometer arrays.
    *   Adept at designing systems for ambient pollution monitoring (NOx, CO) and bicycle detection.
    *   Familiar with environmental performance testing for IT equipment in harsh environments, including drop, topple, and vibration.
    *   Contributed to the design and implementation of the MIDAS (Motorway Incident Detection and Automatic Signalling) subsystem for the M25 London orbital.
    *   Experienced in site pre-commissioning and acceptance trials for highway infrastructure projects.
*   **Power Electronics & Management**
    *   Expert in the design, simulation, and validation of DC/DC converters (Buck, Boost, Buck-Boost, Charge Pumps, SIMO) and power management ICs (PMICs).
    *   Proficient in power conversion and topology design, including bi-directional DC/DC converters and power stages for motor drives.
    *   Skilled in modelling, tuning, and identifying power controller performance, including load/line transients, static efficiency, and ripple analysis.
    *   Experienced in the application of wide bandgap (WBG) semiconductors such as SiC and GaN MOSFETs in power conversion systems.
    *   Adept at designing power distribution networks for complex systems, including multi-phase DC/DC FPGA core supplies.
    *   Competent in managing power for portable battery products, including charging systems and photovoltaic (PV) power integration.
    *   Capable of assessing noise and emissions (EMI) in power electronic circuits and ensuring compliance.
*   **Scientific Instrumentation & Measurements**
    *   Experienced in designing high-precision electronics for NMR and XRF analytical instruments, including temperature controllers and multi-channel histogramming ADCs.
    *   Proficient in developing sensor signal processing chains and conditioning circuitry for various physical and chemical sensors.
    *   Skilled in designing systems for high-speed radiation pulse capture and discrimination, including photomultiplier interfaces and photon counting.
    *   Adept at electrochemical measurements, including potentiostatic control, cyclic voltammetry, and impedance spectroscopy (EIS).
    *   Familiar with various spectroscopic techniques such as FTIR, NMR, XPS, SEM, STEM, and X-Ray for materials characterisation.
    *   Competent in designing and validating interfaces for scientific measurement equipment, ensuring accuracy and stability.
    *   Experienced in developing custom test setups for novel semiconductor materials and scientific applications.
